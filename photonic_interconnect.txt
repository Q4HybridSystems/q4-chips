PHOTONIC INTERCONNECT (SiPh)

Technology: Silicon-on-insulator photonics, 300 mm wafer.
Wavelength division multiplexing: 4 channels per fiber (CWDM4).
Bandwidth: 100 Gbps per channel (400 Gbps aggregate).
Latency: <1 ns per hop (on-package), <10 ns chip-to-chip.
Energy efficiency: <2 pJ/bit.
Modulators: Micro-ring resonators with integrated heaters.
Photodetectors: Germanium waveguide-integrated photodiodes.

Applications:
- CPU-to-AI-accelerator link (Apex Silicon).
- Modem-to-processor interconnect (Eon-X phone).
- Disaggregated data center fabric.

Backdoor resistance: All optical paths are passive; no data
                     can be intercepted without physically
                     tapping the waveguide, which is monitored
                     by PiShield on-chip integrity sensors.
