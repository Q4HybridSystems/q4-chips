ANALOGX1000 MEMRISTOR CROSSBAR

Function: Analog in-memory compute for neural network inference.
Architecture: 1024×1024 resistive RAM (ReRAM) crossbar array.
Precision: 8-bit analog weights; on-chip 10-bit ADC per column.
Performance: 1.2 TOPS/W at 0.5 W per tile.
Process node: 7nm FinFET (compatible with 5nm shrink).
Die area: 12 mm².
Use cases: Edge AI acceleration, real-time sensor processing,
           low-power computer vision, smartphone neural engines.

Key features:
- No external DRAM access during inference (weights stored in situ).
- Differential read scheme for common-mode noise rejection.
- On-chip calibration loop compensates for conductance drift.

Derivative for phones: Scaled 512×512 tile (Eon-X Neural Engine).
